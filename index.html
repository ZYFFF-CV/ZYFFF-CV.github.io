About Me

I am currently an associate professor in the School of Software at Polytechnical University. My research interests include but are not limited to Object detection, Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), and other deep learning applications. I have a rich experience in this field, and I always keep up with the latest research trends. My recent research works include but are not limited to Vision Language Model, Domain-specified Architecture Rebuild, Incremental Learning, and Multimodal learning.

    Research on efficient and accurate object detection algorithms, including deep learning-based object recognition, localization, and tracking techniques.
